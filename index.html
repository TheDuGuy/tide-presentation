The Challenge

From "I own Tide POS" to "I use Tide POS"

  • Tide POS devices are selling well
  • Many merchants still route most volume through competitor devices
  • This is a behaviour / share-of-wallet problem, not a hardware sales problem
  • CRM's job: make Tide the first tap at the point of payment

Strategic Framework

5 CRM strategies to make Tide the first tap

  1. 1 Make Tide the default – Post-purchase "switch" journeys
  2. 2 Win the first £X of volume – Targeted incentive programmes
  3. 3 Build habit at the moment of choice – Behavioural nudges
  4. 4 Use feedback to remove barriers – CX-led journeys
  5. 5 Always-on test & learn – Continuous optimisation & personalisation

Strategy 1

Make Tide the default terminal

Every POS sale should trigger a switch-focused onboarding journey, not just a generic welcome. We want merchants to move from owning Tide POS to using it as their daily default.

GOAL
"I own Tide POS" "I've fully switched to Tide"
  • Trigger: POS device sale or activation
  • Onboarding journey:
    • "How to switch from your old terminal to Tide"
    • Checklist: configure → test → train staff → place device
  • Channels: Email + in-app (optional SMS)

Strategy 2

£0 £250 £500 £750 £1,000
  • Time-bound challenge: "Process your first £1,000 on Tide in 30 days"
  • Test different rewards:
